Biography

Mariana Vazquez is a kitchen lover with a lot of curiosity to discover and create new dishes. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, she always had a passion for cooking. She studied Marketing and Business Administration at the University of Palermo of her hometown and already in her adulthood stage, decided to formally study culinary arts at Cuyahoga Community College, Cleveland, Ohio of the United States, city in which she is based with his Husband and daughters since 2015. It is licensed by the Ohio Department of Agriculture for its cuisine and certified for the proper handling of “Servsafe” food from the “National Restaurant Association” Currently she is fully dedicated to cooking and defines herself as a very restless and enterprising woman. She has a weakness for the argentinian cookie “dulce de leche” sandwiches, “chocotorta” sour cream & caramel chocolate layer cake, “empanadas” and “milanesas” to mention some.
